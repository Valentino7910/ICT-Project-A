My Hobbies

07/03/2024

A few of my favourite hobbies include reading books, watching movies and playing games. My idea of a peaceful Saturday morning involves a book and a hammock (as seen in this photo). Most recently when I went camping, I took this photo while reading The Book of Dust. This is a prequel to one of my favourite book series which I read a while ago and has since been made into a movie! I have yet to watch the movie but its on my list of movies to watch, so I will get around to it one day!

I love watching movies but rarely have the time for it after work or university, it’s nice to sit with my cat, Pantheon, and watch a movie as I fall asleep after a long day but unfortunately this is not conductive to finishing the movies. Hence, I have a list of movies that I have to watch, mostly classic ‘must see’ movies, that I simply never got around to watching.

If I have more energy at the end of the day, you will often find me at my PC gaming with friends online.
